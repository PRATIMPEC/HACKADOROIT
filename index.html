About Us

At Taru, we are driven by a bold vision: to create a world where every woman has the opportunity to thrive. Our mission is clear and unwavering: to work for the creation, protection, and nurturing of a poverty-free, educated, skilled, women-empowered, prosperous, and healthy society. With a foundation built on philanthropic thinking, high governance standards, and best practices, we strive to make a lasting impact across the globe.


Through our holistic approach to empowerment, we address the multifaceted challenges faced by women and girls, from education and economic opportunity to health and human rights. We provide comprehensive support, including access to education, vocational training, healthcare services, and economic resources, empowering women to chart their own course to success.

What we Do

Education and Skill Development:

We believe that education is the cornerstone of empowerment. Through scholarships, mentorship programs, and vocational training, we equip women and girls with the knowledge and skills they need to navigate the complexities of the modern world and pursue their aspirations.

Advocacy and Awareness:

We are staunch advocates for gender equality and social justice. Through targeted campaigns, community dialogues, and policy advocacy, we strive to challenge discriminatory norms, amplify marginalized voices, and effectuate systemic change.

Economic Empowerment:

Economic independence is a key driver of women's empowerment. We facilitate access to financial resources, entrepreneurship training, and market opportunities, enabling women to break free from cycles of poverty, assert their agency, and achieve economic self-sufficiency.

Health and Well-being:

Women's health is a fundamental human right. We work to improve access to reproductive health services, combat gender-based violence, and promote holistic well-being, ensuring that women and girls can lead healthy, fulfilling lives free from fear and stigma.
